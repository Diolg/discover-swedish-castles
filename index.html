Sweden is a country of magical castles

Explore my top-3 favorite castles and make your choice easier!

Why castles are one of the best locations for recreation?

Nowadays, castles have become not just historical attractions, but also destinations where you can have a great time with friends or family within both active and passive recreation. On the territory of modern castles, you can live in a hotel, relax in a spa, taste original cuisine in a restaurant, ride horses, boating, hiking, and much more.

Sweden is a wonderful country where you will find many beautiful castles with different activities in all seasons.

Why exactly these three castles?

I am always of the opinion that before visiting a place, it is worth reading the recommendations and looking at the choices of others. Despite the many castles in Sweden, I chose three of my favorites: Tyreso, Gorvalnslott and Herringe.

You can see breif information about them and find their location on the map here. But before that, find out below what exactly influenced my choice.

How i made my choice?

I am an esthete. It is important for me that the place breathes history and beauty. I love a variety of leisure activities, both active and passive. I appreciate fine cuisine: I like to spend relaxed time in a place with beautiful surroundings and enjoy not only delicious, but also aesthetic food. I just adore nature: it is important for me that the territory allows a lot of walking, swimming, having picnics... And at the end of the day I want to know that I have the opportunity to spend a wonderful night at the castle hotel and enjoy the SPA.

If you share all these ideas, you will understand my choice!
